### Implementation Instructions

1. **Brand Colors**: Change the HEX code in line 42 to match the client's brand colors
2. **Contact Information**: Update phone number and address in the header section
3. **Service Area**: Modify the service area map and coverage zones
4. **Emergency Hours**: Adjust the 24/7 availability messaging based on client operations
5. **Pricing**: Update emergency service rates and standard pricing tiers
6. **Testimonials**: Replace with client-specific customer reviews and ratings

### Key Features

- **Mobile-First Design**: Optimized for emergency calls from mobile devices
- **Fast Loading**: Critical above-the-fold content loads in under 2 seconds
- **Call-to-Action**: Prominent phone number and contact forms
- **Trust Signals**: Licensing, insurance, and certification badges
- **Local SEO**: Schema markup for local plumbing services
